About Bio Gas

About The Smart Bio Gas

This project proposes to increase the productivity and to monitor the usage of biogas by monitoring and controlling the pH value as well as the water level. We will also be monitoring and calculating the bill of total biogas consumption. The purpose is also to spread awareness of causes of stubble burning , how to dispose of their stubble, keep bio degradable and non-bio degradable waste in different dustbins. We will provide information about how much money the farmers will get by selling their stubble, animal manures and other bio-degradable waste; how slurry can be used as fertilizers, labour to collect the stubble.
